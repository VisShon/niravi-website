Places to Explore

3.5 km away

City Palace

As one of the major landmarks in the old city side of Jaipur, City Palace used to be the royal residence and houses a complex of gardens, quarters and buildings.

10 km away

Amber Fort

Also known as Amer Fort, this is one of the main attractions of the city. And a must visit for anyone looking to explore true Rajput & Mughal architecture and history.

7.5 km away

Jal Mahal

Derived from the name, this palace is situated in the middle of Man Sagar lake. And a true design spectacle at night.

1.6 km away

Albert Hall Museum

Fond of history? As one of the oldest museums of the state, Albert Hall is considered one of the best museums for the collection.

2.8 km away

Hawa Mahal

Built with the Jaipur trademark of red and pink sandstone, the Hawa Mahal is famous for its picturesque landscape. It lies at the edge of the City Palace

13 km away

Jaigarh Fort

Situated on Cheel ka Teela or Hill of Eagles, this fort was built to protect the Amer Palace. It houses Jaivana, which was then considered the world's largest cannon on wheels.

1.2 km away

Birla Temple

Constructed of only white marble, this religious establishment dedicated to the Hindu goddess Lakshmi and god Vishnu (Narayan) was built in 1988.
