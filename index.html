NIRAVI JAIPUR

An enchanting heritage retreat tucked away in the heart of Jaipur

Located in the center of Jaipur’s old city, Niravi Jaipur is a boutique haven nestled amidst nature and tranquility. Perfect for solo travelers esp women, couples, families, and groups looking to relax, refresh, and reconnect with life.

Live through the unparalleled legacy of Rajasthani hospitality like never before

Our rooms

Bougainvillea

Named after the stunning Bougainvillea flower, these spacious rooms are located on the first floor and feature a private terrace with views of the pool.

Madhukamini

These rooms draw their name from the enchanting Madhukamini (Orange Jasmine), they offer stunning pool views and feature expansive interiors, including spacious bathrooms.

Bela

Inspired by the Bela flower, known for its exquisite white blossoms and delightful fragrance, these rooms are situated in the new wing and offer captivating pool views.

Gulmohar

The name is inspired by Gulmohar tree, renowned for its vibrant hues. These rooms offer a picturesque pool and surrounding views.

Raat ki Raani

These rooms are named after the "Raat ki Raani" (Queen of the Night), promising a delightful olfactory experience with its fragrant flowering bushes nearby.

Sevra

Inspired by the Sevra plant, known for its vibrant green leaves, these rooms are situated on the first floor of the heritage bungalow.
